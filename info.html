Shri Sangameshwar Education Society's,
Sangameshwar College,Solapur
was established in June 1953 by Karmayogi: Late Shri. N. B. Kadadi and Late Shri. M. B. Kadadi.
This institute is standing splendid and secrene at the historical saat rasta chowk, Solapur, bearing more than 5 decades of testimony to higher education.
